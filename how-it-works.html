What FederalPathAI Does

FederalPathAI is an AI-powered assistant designed to help federal job seekers draft USAJOBS-ready resumes aligned with OPM guidance and vacancy announcements. It focuses on creating concise, two-page resumes that highlight specialized experience, competencies, and impact.

How to Use FederalPathAI

  1. Find a federal job announcement. Copy the full text or URL from USAJOBS.
  2. Gather your experience. Collect your prior roles, accomplishments, and dates.
  3. Open the FederalPathAI app. Paste your vacancy details and your experience when prompted.
  4. Generate a draft resume. The AI will create a 2-page federal-style resume based on your input.
  5. Review and refine. Read through the draft, correct any details, and customize where needed.
  6. Download or copy. Move the final text into your preferred document editor for formatting and submission.

What FederalPathAI Is and Is Not

FederalPathAI is:

  • A drafting assistant for federal-style resumes.
  • A tool to help you align your experience to vacancy language.
  • Focused on clarity, impact, and specialized experience.

FederalPathAI is not:

  • A guarantee of selection, referral, or hiring.
  • A replacement for your own judgment and review.
  • A legal, HR, or official OPM authority.

Best Practices When Using the Tool

  • Always confirm that dates, grades, and job titles are accurate.
  • Ensure your resume reflects your true experience and qualifications.
  • Compare the output to the vacancy announcement and adjust as needed.
  • Keep a copy of each resume version you submit for your records.
